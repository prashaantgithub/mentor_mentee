About the Program

Our Mentor Connect Program is designed to foster meaningful relationships between students and experienced mentors from our faculty, alumni, and senior student body. We believe that mentorship is a powerful tool for personal and professional growth, helping students navigate their academic journey and prepare for a successful future.

Who Can Join?

For Students

Are you a junior student looking to get ahead? Navigate your academic path, build a professional network early, and gain a trusted advisor to guide you through your college journey.

For Mentors

Are you a faculty member, senior student, or alumnus? Guide the next generation, share your hard-earned wisdom, and make a lasting impact on a student's career and life.

Key Benefits

Academic Guidance

Get help with course selection, study strategies, and academic challenges.

Career Advice

Explore career paths, get resume feedback, and prepare for interviews.

Mental Wellness Support

Connect with someone who understands college pressures and can offer support.

Alumni Networking

Build connections with successful alumni in your field of interest.
